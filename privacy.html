Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

We keep this simple. If you enter your email in the early access form on our landing page, we store that email address in your browser's localStorage. That is the only piece of personal information we collect through our website.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, IP addresses, device fingerprints, or any other identifying information through our site.

How We Use It

Your email address is used for one purpose: to notify you when new Zaphenath products launch or when early access becomes available. We will never send unsolicited marketing, and every email will include an unsubscribe option.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. Our analytics provider is Plausible, which is privacy-first and cookie-free. Plausible does not collect personal data, does not track users across sites, and is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR without requiring a cookie banner.

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party. The only external services involved are:

  • Plausible Analytics — anonymous, aggregated traffic data only (no personal data)
  • Vercel — hosts our website (standard server logs may apply under their own privacy policy)
  • GitHub — hosts our open source repositories (their privacy policy applies when you visit GitHub)

Data Retention

Email addresses stored in localStorage persist until you clear your browser data. We do not maintain a server-side database of collected emails at this time. If we introduce server-side storage in the future, we will update this policy and notify existing subscribers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your localStorage at any time to remove your email from our site
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Ask us what data we have associated with your email
  • Opt out of any future communications

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email us at hello@zaphenath.app.
